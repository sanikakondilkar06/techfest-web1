What is IETE?

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is a premier professional society advancing futuristic tech, learning, and innovation in electronics, IT, and communications. Bridging visionaries, learners, and industry leaders‚ÄîIETE is where ideas and talent ignite.

The Genesis

In 2008, the IETE-KJSIT student chapter was founded, marking the beginning of a new era of technical innovation and community building at the institute.

First National Event

By 2012, we hosted our first national-level technical event, "Renaissance," establishing a platform for collaboration and setting a new benchmark for excellence.

Community Growth

Our community grew to over 5,000 members by 2018, becoming a major force in empowering students with skills and opportunities for the future.

A Legacy of Excellence

By 2023, we celebrated a legacy of successfully organizing over 50 events, redefining what a student-run technical body can achieve through dedication and passion.
